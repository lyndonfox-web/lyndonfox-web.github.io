My name is Lyndon Fox

Kia Ora, my name is Lyndon Fox. Welcome to my website. I am a web developer based in Christchurch. My experience encompasses different web languages such as html, CSS, jQuery, JavaScript, Vue.js, PHP and Wordpress. As a web designer, I have enlarged my scope of business venturing into projects that I can contribute as a Graphic Designer, Artist and Illustrator.

I obtained my Diploma in Web and UX Design from Yoobee and my Diploma in Graphic Design from Design Arts College of New Zealand.
